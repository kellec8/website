Welcome!

Climbing is an amazing sport that allows for you to challenge yourself while taking part in a very supportive community. In addition, climbing is also a very physical and technical sport, allowing you to get exercise in a fun way!

However, climbing can be intimidating to get into, as there are many different forms of climbing, and it's often not a sport people have much experience with, when starting out.

That being said, I hope that I can help you learn a bit about the sport and some of the ways that people climb! You can get started by selecting any of the three types listed below.
